## Tips for every article

1. **Target one primary keyword** — put it in the H1, URL slug, and first paragraph
2. **Add an H2 that mirrors the search query** — e.g. "How to merge PDF files free (step by step)"
3. **Include a numbered step section** — Google often pulls this into featured snippets
4. **Add FAQ schema** to each article (see schema file)
5. **Link to the tool** at least 2–3 times per article with descriptive anchor text
6. **Aim for 800–1,200 words** — enough to be thorough, not padded
7. **Add a "Last updated" date** and keep it accurate
